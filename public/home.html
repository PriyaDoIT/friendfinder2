About Our App

Friend Finder is an easy way to make new friends at your own convenience. Forget awkward conversations at the bar and spending money on expensive social groups. Friend Finder's algorithms are proven to help you find your next best friend.

How The App Works?

Enter your name

It can be your full name, nickname, or whatever you want to go by.

Upload

Upload a photo of yourself. Try to choose a picture that is an accurate depicton of what you really look like (no edited photos). Tip: find a picture that displays you doing something that you enjoy. For example, exploring the outdoors, dining at your favorite restaurant, or doing your favorite activity.

Answer Questions

Answer a few questions about yourself on a scale of 1 to 5, with 1 being you strongly disagree to the statement and 5 being your strongly agreeing to the statement presented. Be honest with yourself.

Find your Friend

Once you answer all of the required information, Friend Finder will work its magic. Friend Finder's sophisticated algorithms are proven to match you with those who are the most compatible to your personality type.
